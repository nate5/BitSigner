Why is it dangerous to be connected to the internet?

Being offline ensures that even if you have a malicious version of this app, you cannot be deanonymized.

If you were connected to the internet and had a malicious version of this app, the app could use Javascript to deanonymize you.

How do I get offline?

To disconnect from the internet in the Tor Browser Bundle, follow these steps

1, go to the menu (top right corner)
2, go to the "Developer" tab
3, click "Work Offline"

Although this is a safe way to disconnect, the safest way would to be physically disconnect the internet from your computer.
